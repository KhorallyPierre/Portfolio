About Me

In middle school I knew very little English, and as a result I gravitated towards turning the scenes into my head into a reality through drawing. My sketch book was a safe haven because I loved to breathe life into the ideas in my head. It amazed me that I could draw inspiration, and create something new on a blank sheet of paper. I then gained a strong desire to put together words to go along with my images, which caused me to fall in love with language.

With a keyboard under my fingers, and computer languages like JavaScript, MongoDB, and others within my reach, I am ready to create from the ground up.
